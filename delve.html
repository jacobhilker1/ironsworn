Delve the Depths

Delve Move

When you explore a perilous site in search of an objective, first envision the nature of this place (Ask the Oracle if unsure), and set its rank.
  • Troublesome site: 3 progress per area.
  • Dangerous site: 2 progress per area.
  • Formidable site: 1 progress per area.
  • Extreme site: 2 ticks per area.
  • Epic site: 1 tick per area.

Then, for each area you traverse, envision your surroundings (Ask the Oracle if unsure). If you navigate this area…

  • With haste, roll +edge
  • With stealth or trickery, roll +shadow
  • With observation, intuition, or expertise, roll +wits

On a strong hit, you delve deeper. Mark progress and Discover an Opportunity.

On a weak hit, roll on the following according to your stat.

Discover an Opportunity

Delve Move

When you encounter a helpful situation within a site, roll on the following. If you are making this move as a result of a strong hit on Delve the Depths, you may choose one instead of rolling.
Roll Result
1-2 You cross paths with someone who is inclined to support you. If you Compel them or Gather Information from them, add +2.
3-5 You encounter someone in need of help. If you act to aid them, add +2 when you first make a move to intercede.
6-8 You encounter something which bolsters your resolve. You may Secure an Advantage by reaffirming your commitment to your current vow. If you do, add +1. If you instead Swear an Iron Vow to undertake a new quest, add +2.
9-18 You locate a secure area. If you take a breather, you and your allies may take +1 health. If you instead Make Camp, add +2.
19-28 This area provides an opportunity to scavenge, forage, or hunt. If you Resupply, add +2.
29-38 A clue offers insight into your quest. If you Gather Information, add +2.
39-48 You get the drop on a potential foe. If you Enter the Fray, add +2.
49-69 You encounter something which offers insight into the history or nature of this place. Envision what you learn (Ask the Oracle if unsure). You and your allies may take +1 momentum.
70-00 The terrain favors you, or you find a hidden path. If you push forward, add +1 when you Delve the Depths. If you instead Escape the Depths, add +2.

Locate Your Objective

Delve (Progress) Move

When your exploration of a site comes to an end, roll the challenge dice and compare to your progress. Momentum is ignored on this roll.

On a strong hit, you locate your objective and the situation favors you. Choose one.

  • Make another move now (not a progress move), and add +1.
  • Take +1 momentum.

On a weak hit, you locate your objective but face an unforeseen hazard or complication. Envision what you find (Ask the Oracle if unsure).

On a miss, your objective falls out of reach, you have been misled about the nature of your objective, or you discover that this site holds unexpected depths. If you continue your exploration, clear all but one filled progress and raise the site’s rank by one (if not already epic). Otherwise, Escape the Depths and do what you must to find a path forward.

Escape the Depths

Delve Move

When you flee or withdraw from a site, roll +wits.

On a strong hit, you make your way safely out of this place. Take +2 momentum.

On a weak hit, you find your way out, but this place exacts its price. You and your allies must each choose one.

  • You are worn and weary: Endure Harm (2 harm)
  • The experience has left you you shaken: Endure Stress (2 stress)
  • It takes longer than you expected: Suffer -2 momentum.

On a miss, this place hinders your departure. You must Reveal a Danger. If you survive, you may attempt to Escape the Depths again.

Check your Gear

Delve Move

When you check to see if you have a particular helpful item, roll +supply.

On a strong hit, you have it. Take +2 momentum.

On a weak hit, choose one.

  • You have it: Take +2 momentum but suffer -1 supply.)
  • You’ll have to make do without it: Take +1 momentum.

On a miss, you don’t have it and the situation grows more perilous. Pay the Price.
